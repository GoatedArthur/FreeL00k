FreeL00k adds a full 360-degree freelook camera to Minecraft Forge 1.8.9.

Hold Left Alt (default, rebindable) to freely rotate your view without changing your movement direction -- perfect for scouting ahead, checking your surroundings while running, or using a third-person perspective with full camera control.

Unlike basic freelook mods, FreeL00k syncs the camera position with your look direction, so the view stays realistic around corners and blocks. The player model stays frozen -- no head or body rotation.

Features:
- Full 360 horizontal and vertical rotation (no pitch limit)
- Configurable max yaw: 90, 180, or 360 degrees
- Hold or toggle mode
- Choose your default perspective: keep current, third-person back, or third-person front
- Works with inverted mouse
- Config GUI in the Forge mod list
- Player model stays completely frozen during freelook
- Proper camera positioning for realistic views around obstacles

I recommend using third-person back (F5) perspective. Bind the key in Controls > FreeL00k.
